Quick Setup Guide
Follow these steps to monetize your flip book site with Google AdSense.
  • 1 Sign up or log in at google.com/adsense and add your website URL to your AdSense account.
  • 2 Go to AdSense → Ads → By ad unit and create a new Display ad unit. Copy the full <script> code they give you.
  • 3 Paste that code into the AdSense & Ads section of this dashboard. It will automatically appear at the bottom of every story page.
  • 4 Go to AdSense → Account → ads.txt and copy the line they provide. Paste it into the ads.txt Manager section of this dashboard.
  • 5 Download the updated ads.txt file from this dashboard and upload it to the root of your hosting (same folder as index.html).

How to Upload ads.txt
  • 1 Paste your AdSense ads.txt line above and click Download ads.txt File.
  • 2 Upload the downloaded ads.txt file to the root folder of your website hosting — the same folder where your index.html lives.
  • 3 Verify it's working by visiting yourdomain.com/ads.txt in your browser. You should see the content you pasted.
  • 4 Google AdSense will automatically detect the file within 24–48 hours and verify your site ownership.
