How to Upload ads.txt
  • 1Paste your AdSense ads.txt line above and click Download ads.txt File.
  • 2Upload the downloaded ads.txt file to the root folder of your website hosting — the same folder where your index.html lives.
  • 3Verify it's working by visiting stories.cynthiashortstoryhub.com/ads.txt in your browser.
  • 4Google AdSense will automatically detect the file within 24–48 hours.
